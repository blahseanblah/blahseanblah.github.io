ABOUT

SAFE+SOUND is a day for the community to join together and learn how to take action to protect ourselves and each other.

During the day, we will offer free self-defense training, where self-defense experts will teach skills such as recognizing boundaries, de-escalation tactics, light physical techniques to get away from an attacker, and ways to safely respond when you witness harassment or a crime against another person. During this time, we will also have nonprofit organizations present to provide additional resources and support, as well as connect with community members who are interested in getting involved.

In the evening after the self-defense training, we will open the doors again for a show featuring a lineup of local musicians who inspire us - so we can celebrate equality, respect, and community empowerment. Proceeds from the door will support the nonprofits who will be at the event, as well as ensure that the training can be offered for free to our community.
